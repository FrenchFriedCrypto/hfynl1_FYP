USD Costs & Savings for Providing Liquidity

For x tokens:

Dual Liquidity Cost = 1000 × 2 × (x C 2) = 1000 × x × (x – 1)
Multicoin Liquidity Cost = 1000 × x
Savings = 1 – (Multicoin Cost / Dual Cost), expressed as a percentage.
